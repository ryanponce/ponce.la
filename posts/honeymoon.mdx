Sandy and I went to Maui, Hawaii for our honeymoon. We stayed at the
Westin in Kaanapali. Here are some of the cool things we did with a bunch
of photos (which are all shot on film).

- Monkeypod's happy hour
- Trilogy excursion
- Shaved ice in Lahaina (twice)
- Got a private cabana on the beach for a day
- Ate a true fried McDonald's apple pie (apparently this is one of the few
  that still fried them)
- Mama's fish house
- Snorkled
- Scraped our rental car
- Chilled hard
